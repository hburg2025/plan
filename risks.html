First things first!

Before anything else, please:

  1. Be smart about personal information! You will notice we don't ask for any information on this site. You should be suspecious of anyone asking for detailed personal information, even if they seem like they want to help or belong to a community organization. Also, try to consider the risk that people you share sensitive personal information with could be targeted by an ICE raid themselves: is it low risk, or higher risk?
