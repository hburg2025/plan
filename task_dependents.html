Who can take care of people who depend on you?

The kind of things you might ask for someone to help with are:

  • You need someone to take care of your child. Choose a "trusted helper" and share the following information with them (for each child):
    • Full name, and date of birth
    • Medical conditions, allergies, prescriptions for medicine, pharmacy information, doctor information, insurance information
    • School, extracurricular activities
    • Babysitter contact information
    • Favorite toy, favorite food, other notes.
  • You need someone to take care of an adult dependent. Choose a "trusted helper" and share the following information with them (for each dependent):
    • Full name, and date of birth
    • Medical conditions, allergies, prescriptions for medicine, pharmacy information, doctor information, insurance information
