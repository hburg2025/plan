Dear Friend, let's make a plan...

We are community members, concerned about the way our town and country are changing. We include social workers, professors, lawyers, business owners, faith leaders, and many other people who want to help.

This letter is to each "undocumented" member of our community.

We value you-- you are part of our community. As we both know, though, actions by U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) and related groups are increasing to take "undocumented" people into custody and disrupt your family systems. To minimize family disruption, in the event of an ICE raid, you will need to plan ahead. No plan will be perfect, but having some hard conversations now could make a big difference later on.

In this site, you'll hear from other undocumented immigrant individuals and families who have survived ICE raids and want to pass on what worked and what didn't. We'll try to provide checklists that you can use to help think through what needs to be decided. And we'll update these as we learn more, so check back periodically.
